Class ReagentGrinderComponent

The combo reagent grinder/juicer. The reason why grinding and juicing are seperate is simple, think of grinding as a utility to break an object down into its reagents. Think of juicing as converting something into its single juice form. E.g, grind an apple and get the nutriment and sugar it contained, juice an apple and get "apple juice".
